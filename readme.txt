To move the robot, press 'm', then use the arrow keys to move
To enter rotation mode, press 'r', then use the left and right arrow keys to rotate
Also supports mouse wheel rotations.

To rotate a segment, click on that segment, then rotate the mouse wheel, use the arrow keys or use the left and right mouse clicks to rotate.

Click on the claws to open or close the claws.

If the robot collides with a piece of space junk, the robot is placed back at the starting location and a dialog box will pop out.
